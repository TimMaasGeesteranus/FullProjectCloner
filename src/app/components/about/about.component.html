Hogeschool van Arnhem en Nijmegen (HAN) / University of Applied Science

In general

HAN is one of the largest universities of applied sciences in the Netherlands. It manages more than 3500 employees within the HAN from the campuses in Arnhem and Nijmegen, over 35.000 students, 64 bachelor programs and 18 masters in the variants full-time, part-time and dual. In addition, the HAN offers 14 associate degrees and approximately 300 courses and training courses. HAN also applies research, including 36 professorships and 7 centers of expertise on knowledge innovation.

Informatics Communication Academy (ICA)

In general

The Informatics Communication Academy (ICA) provides higher professional education and master programs at the HAN University of Applied Sciences in the field of informatics, digital media and communication.

Practically oriented

With more than 1600 students and approximately 120 employees the Informatica Communication Academy in Gelderland is seen as the beating heart of higher education for future Bachelors of ICT. The academy stands for a well-considered mix of IT and Communication embedded in a practical approach. The student states that due to this working method, he/she gets a considerate practical insight of how things are done to meet the market requirements.
